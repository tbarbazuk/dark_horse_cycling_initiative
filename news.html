FLE #5 Hammock Hauler at San Felasco, Alachua Fl

June 15, 2025

Team president Thomas Barbazuk entered the open sprint category at the 5th race of the Florida Endurance series brought to you by Gone riding (www.goneriding.com) at San Felasco Canopy Preserve in Alachau Fl. Conditions were dry and dusty on the home trails. A hot 30 miles of single track saw Thomas finish in 4th place, just off the podium.
